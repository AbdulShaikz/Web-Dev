BMI Calculator

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.

1. Enter your weight and height using metric measures.

2. Select "Compute" and your BMI will appear below.

BMI Categories:
                Underweight <= 18.5
                Normal weight = 18.5 - 24.9
                Overweight = 25 - 29.9
                Obesity = BMI of 30 or greater
